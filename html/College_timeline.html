Top 5 Priorities to Achieve Actuarial Career Goals

Pass Actuarial Exam P

Reason: Essential for starting your actuarial career.

  • Register for the exam and dedicate weekly study hours.
  • Complete practice exams with a minimum score of 80%.
  • Use trusted resources like ASM study manuals.

Secure an Actuarial Internship

Reason: Gain practical experience in the field.

  • Apply to at least 15 internships by January 2025.
  • Network at SOA career fairs for visibility.
  • Prepare resumes and cover letters tailored to roles.

Earn Professional Certifications

Reason: Enhance technical expertise and marketability.

  • Focus on certifications like CompTIA Data+ or Azure.
  • Complete one certification per quarter starting January 2025.
  • Use online platforms for guided study.

Build a Technical Portfolio

Reason: Showcase applied knowledge through projects.

  • Create 3-5 projects demonstrating technical skills.
  • Leverage tools like Python, Tableau, and Excel VBA.
  • Host the portfolio on your website by May 2025.

Graduate from College Programs

Reason: Fulfill academic credentials for a strong foundation.

  • Complete coursework with a GPA of 3.5+.
  • Dedicate 20 hours weekly to studies and projects.
  • Meet graduation requirements by Fall 2025.
